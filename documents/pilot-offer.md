# Legal Intake Assistant – 30-Day Pilot

## Improve Client Response Without Adding Staff

Prospective clients contact your firm at all hours. When your team is unavailable, potential clients move on. Our intake assistant ensures every inquiry receives an immediate, professional response—capturing leads, answering questions from your approved content, and booking consultations.

---

## What the Pilot Includes

**Intake Assistant Configuration**
- Trained exclusively on your firm's website content, FAQs, and approved materials
- Configured with your practice areas, jurisdictions, and office information

**General Information Responses**
- Answers common questions using your approved content
- Every response includes a citation to the source
- Clear, professional communication aligned with your brand voice

**Lead Capture and Routing**
- Collects name, contact information, and case summary
- Delivers leads via email or integrates with your CRM
- Includes conversation transcript with each lead

**Consultation Booking**
- Integrates with Calendly, Acuity, or your scheduling system
- Books consultations directly during the conversation
- Confirms appointments and provides next steps

**Guardrails and Refusal Logic**
- Refuses to provide legal advice, eligibility assessments, or outcome predictions
- Routes complex questions to your intake team or consultation
- Logs all refusals for your review

**Light Tuning**
- Adjustments based on real conversations during the pilot
- Refinements to response accuracy and tone

---

## What the Pilot Does NOT Include

- **No legal advice** – The assistant provides general information only
- **No eligibility determinations** – "Do I have a case?" questions are routed to consultation
- **No strategy or case assessment** – All case-specific questions require attorney consultation
- **No outcome predictions** – The assistant does not estimate results or timelines
