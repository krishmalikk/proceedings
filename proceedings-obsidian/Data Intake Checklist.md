# Data Intake Checklist

**Location:** `/documents/data-intake-checklist.md`

---

## Purpose

Client onboarding form that collects all information needed to configure the intake assistant for a specific law firm.

---

## Sections

1. **Firm Information** — Name, contact, practice areas, jurisdictions
2. **Website Access** — Domain, sitemap, pages to include/exclude
3. **Approved Content Sources** — Website content, additional documents, FAQs
4. **Intake and Routing** — Lead delivery (email/CRM), consultation booking tool
5. **Office Details** — Hours, locations, languages supported
6. **Disallowed Topics** — Standard exclusions (legal advice, eligibility, etc.) + firm-specific
7. **Brand Voice** — Tone, greeting style, firm name usage
8. **Privacy Rules** — Allowed vs disallowed data collection (no SSN, DL, financial accounts)
9. **Escalation Rules** — Keywords triggering attorney referral, automatic refusal scenarios
10. **Go-Live Acceptance Criteria** — 10 standard test questions with expected behaviors
11. **Technical Requirements** — Deployment method, website platform, technical contact
12. **Signatures** — Authorization to use content, no legal advice acknowledgment
