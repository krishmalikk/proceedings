## What's Included

- Assistant trained on firm's website, FAQs, and approved materials
- General information responses with source citations
- Lead capture (name, contact, case summary) delivered via email or CRM
- Consultation booking (Calendly, Acuity, or other)
- Guardrails and refusal logic with logged refusals
- Light tuning based on real conversations

---

## What's Excluded

- Legal advice or opinions
- Eligibility determinations
- Strategy or case assessment
- Outcome predictions
